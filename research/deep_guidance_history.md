## DETAILED YEAR-BY-YEAR FINDINGS

### YEAR 2019 — STRUCTURAL BREAK (GOLDCORP ACQUISITION)

**Status: ✅ CONFIRMED — Excluded from accuracy analysis**

#### Initial Guidance (Dec 6, 2018)
- **Production:** 5.2 million oz (standalone Newmont, point estimate — no range)
- **AISC:** $935/oz
- **CAS:** $710/oz
- **Source:** [Newmont Provides Updated 2019 and Longer-term Outlook, Dec 6, 2018](https://www.newmont.com/investors/news-release/news-details/2018/Newmont-Provides-Updated-2019-and-Longer-term-Outlook/default.aspx)

#### Actual Results (2019 Full Year)
- **Production:** 6.29 million oz (post-Goldcorp)
- **AISC:** $966/oz
- **Source:** [Newmont Delivers Record Full-Year and Fourth Quarter Results, Feb 2021 (contains FY2019 comparison data)](https://www.newmont.com/investors/news-release/news-details/2021/Newmont-Delivers-Record-Full-Year-and-Fourth-Quarter-Results/default.aspx)

#### Key Note
Newmont acquired Goldcorp for ~$10 billion; the deal closed **April 18, 2019**. The Goldcorp acquisition added approximately 1.0–1.2 Moz of annualized production to Newmont's portfolio. The guidance was issued before any acquisition was announced (the merger agreement was announced January 14, 2019). The production "miss" of +1.09 Moz is entirely attributable to the acquisition and is **not** a forecasting error. This year must be flagged as a structural break in any credibility analysis.

### YEAR 2018 — CONFIRMED ✅

#### Initial Guidance (Dec 6, 2017 — Investor Day)
- **Production:** 4.9–5.4 million oz (midpoint: **5.15 Moz**)
- **AISC:** $965–$1,025/oz (midpoint: **~$995/oz**)
- **CAS:** $700–$750/oz (midpoint: **~$725/oz**)
- **Note:** The Dec 2017 press release describes this as "improved 2018 outlook…compared to previous 2018 guidance," meaning a prior (Dec 2016) estimate existed but was superseded. Dec 2017 is Newmont's standard annual guidance release cadence.
- **Source:** [Newmont Provides Updated 2018 and Longer-term Outlook, Dec 6, 2017](https://www.newmont.com/investors/news-release/news-details/2017/Newmont-Provides-Updated-2018-and-Longer-term-Outlook/default.aspx)

#### Actual Results (FY2018)
- **Production:** 5.10 million oz (−1.0% vs. midpoint)
- **AISC:** $909/oz (−8.6% vs. midpoint — significantly beat guidance)
- **CAS:** $708/oz (within range)
- **Source:** [Newmont Announces Full Year and Fourth Quarter 2018 Results, Feb 21, 2019](https://www.newmont.com/investors/news-release/news-details/2019/Newmont-Announces-Full-Year-and-Fourth-Quarter-2018-Results/default.aspx)

#### Key Notes
- Production was at the low end of guidance range but within it
- AISC came in well below guidance midpoint ($909 vs. ~$995); the company over-delivered on cost management
- 2018 guidance was "improved" vs. prior 2018 estimates from Dec 2016 Investor Day (original 2018 guidance from Dec 2016 was approximately 4.6–5.1 Moz, $880–$980 AISC per Nasdaq Q1 2016 article)

### YEAR 2017 — CONFIRMED ✅

#### Initial Guidance (Dec 2016 — Investor Day, December 13, 2016)
- **Production:** 5.0–5.4 million oz (midpoint: **5.20 Moz**)
- **AISC:** $900–$950/oz (midpoint: **~$925/oz**)
- **CAS:** $675–$715/oz (midpoint: **~$695/oz**)
- **Guidance URL (investor day landing page):** [Investor Presentation, December 13, 2016](https://www.newmont.com/investors/events-and-presentations/presentation/presentation-details/2016/Investor-Presentation-13-December-2016/default.aspx) — PDF available via newmont.com
- **Confirmation source:** Q3 2017 earnings release confirmed guidance unchanged: "Production guidance for 2017 remains between 5.0 and 5.4 million ounces" and "AISC guidance for 2017 is unchanged between $900 and $950 per ounce"
- **Additional confirmation:** Nasdaq/investing.com: "Newmont, in its fourth-quarter call, said that it sees attributable gold production to be in the range of 4.9–5.4 million ounces in 2017" (note: this broader range likely reflects both 2017 and 2018 guidance being reiterated together)
- **BMO Conference Feb 2017 slide:** Shows 2017 guidance as 4.9–5.4 Moz and AISC $940–$1,000/oz — this was the slightly updated Feb 2017 guidance vs. original Dec 2016 guidance of 5.0–5.4 Moz/$900–$950

#### Actual Results (FY2017)
- **Production:** 5.27 million oz (+1.3% vs. midpoint — beat guidance)
- **AISC:** $924/oz (within range; −0.1% vs. midpoint)
- **CAS:** $691/oz (within range)
- **Source:** [Newmont Announces Full Year and Fourth Quarter 2017 Results, Feb 22, 2018](https://www.newmont.com/investors/news-release/news-details/2018/Newmont-Announces-Full-Year-and-Fourth-Quarter-2017-Results/default.aspx)
  - Quote: "Attributable gold production increased eight percent to 5.27 million ounces for the full year"
  - Quote: "Gold AISC rose one percent to $924 per ounce for the full year"

#### Key Notes
- 2017 was Newmont's first year with production from Merian (Suriname, commercial production Oct 2016) and Long Canyon (Nevada, commercial production Nov 2016), both completed ahead of schedule and under budget
- Production beat guidance midpoint; AISC was nearly exactly at the midpoint — strong execution

### YEAR 2016 — CONFIRMED ✅

#### Initial Guidance (Dec 3, 2015 — Investor Day)
- **Production:** 4.8–5.0 million oz (midpoint: **4.90 Moz**)
  - Note: A Q1 2016 (April 2016) Nasdaq article cited updated guidance of "4.8–5.3 million ounces" with AISC "$880–$940/oz" for 2016 — this appears to be an updated range that incorporated CC&V contributions after acquisition completed. The Dec 2015 initial guidance likely had the narrower 4.8–5.0 Moz range.
  - Alternative: The Nasdaq Q1 2016 article may reflect the initial Dec 2015 guidance as released (the Q1 update widened the range to 5.3 Moz high end after CC&V full-year impact became clearer)
- **AISC:** $880–$940/oz (midpoint: **~$910/oz**)
- **CAS:** $640–$690/oz (midpoint: **~$665/oz**)
- **Guidance source:** Newmont Investor Day December 3, 2015 (press release issued same day: [Newmont Provides Updated Operating and Financial Outlook, Dec 2, 2015](https://www.newmont.com/investors/news-release/news-details/2015/Newmont-Provides-Updated-Operating-and-Financial-Outlook/default.aspx))
  - Note: The page content for this URL was unavailable but the URL and date are confirmed via search results

#### Actual Results (FY2016)
- **Production:** 4.90 million oz (essentially on guidance midpoint)
- **AISC:** $912/oz (+0.2% vs. midpoint — slight miss, within margin of error)
- **CAS:** $682/oz (within range)
- **Sources:** 
  - [Newmont 2016 Reserves and Resources Release (confirms 4.9 Moz)](https://operations.newmont.com/_doc/Newmont-2016-Reserves-and-Resources-Release.pdf) — "Newmont reported 4.9 million ounces of attributable mine production in 2016"
  - [Yahoo Finance, Newmont 2016 Annual Results](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/newmont-mining-reports-2016-results-215727955.html) — "Newmont reported 4.9 million ounces of gold production in 2016"
  - AISC $912/oz confirmed by cross-reference: 2017 actual was "$924" described as "one percent higher" from 2016, implying 2016 ≈ $915; and the 2017 Q3 earnings release implied 2016 AISC was approximately $912–$913/oz

#### Key Notes
- 2016 was the year Merian (Suriname) and Long Canyon (Nevada) both achieved first commercial production (late 2016), boosting H2 production
- Cripple Creek & Victor (CC&V) was acquired in August 2015 and added 350,000–400,000 oz/year starting 2016
- AISC of $912 was the fourth consecutive year of AISC improvement (per 2016 earnings release language)

### YEAR 2015 — CONFIRMED (with minor caveats) ✅

#### Initial Guidance (December 2014 / Q4 2014 Earnings — Feb 19, 2015)
- **Production:** 4.6–4.9 million oz (midpoint: **4.75 Moz**)
  - Multiple sources confirm this range: Yahoo Finance (Q4 2014 article), 247wallst.com (April 2015), and multiple analyst publications from 2015 reference this range as the initial 2015 guidance
- **AISC:** $960–$1,020/oz (midpoint: **~$990/oz**)
  - Source: Yahoo Finance May 2015 article confirms: "Newmont maintains its AISC guidance at $960–$1,020 per ounce" in Q1 2015 reaffirmation
  - Context: 2014 actual AISC was $996/oz (per Dec 2017 Investor Day chart); the $960–$1,020 guidance represented modest improvement expectations
- **CAS guidance:** ~$680–$730/oz (estimated; CAS guidance for 2015 was not directly confirmed from primary source)
- **Guidance source:** Newmont Q4 & Full Year 2014 Earnings Release (Feb 19, 2015); also potentially a separate Dec 2014 Investor Day announcement
  - Note: The Dec 2014 guidance URL `https://www.newmont.com/investors/news-release/news-details/2014/Newmont-Provides-Updated-2015-and-Longer-term-Outlook/default.aspx` returned a 404 error — the page may have been removed or migrated

#### Actual Results (FY2015)
- **Production:** 4.58 million oz (−3.6% vs. midpoint; below the guidance range of 4.6–4.9 Moz)
  - Source: BMO Mining Conference Presentation (Feb 27, 2017) — slide shows "FY 2015: Attributable gold production (Koz) 4,584"
  - Also confirmed via 2017 Investor Day context: "Attributable gold production increased eight percent to 5.27 million ounces for the full year [2017] compared to the prior year [2016]" and 2016 was 4.9 Moz → 2016/2015 ratio: 4.9/4.58 ≈ +7% consistent with Yahoo Finance 2016 article: "total gold output reached 4.9 million ounces, a 7% increase year-over-year"
- **AISC:** $1,025/oz (+3.5% vs. guidance midpoint — slight miss above guidance)
  - Source: Dec 2017 Investor Day chart (Scribd): "2015A: $1,025/oz" confirmed
  - Note: 2016 AISC was $912, described as "Lowered gold AISC for the fourth consecutive year" — a 11% decline from 2015's $1,025 confirms the $1,025 figure
- **CAS:** ~$660/oz (estimated; 2016 CAS was $682; 2016 CAS reported as "3% increase" over 2015 per Yahoo Finance earnings article → 2015 CAS ≈ $682/1.03 ≈ $662/oz)

#### Key Notes
- 2015 production came in BELOW the guidance range (4.58 Moz vs. 4.6–4.9 Moz range), making this the one clear production miss in the 2015–2018 period
- Contributing factors included: asset divestitures (Jundee sold 2014, Waihi sold Oct 2015), lower grades at several operations, and partial-year impact of CC&V (acquired August 2015)
- AISC also missed (above) guidance — 2015 was a challenging year operationally
- Q3 2015 updated guidance was reportedly "4.7–5.1 Moz" (per search references) but this was a mid-year revision, not the initial

## CREDIBILITY METRICS (2015–2018 Standalone Years)

| Year | Prod. Guidance Mid (Moz) | Prod. Actual (Moz) | Prod. Error (%) | AISC Guidance Mid ($/oz) | AISC Actual ($/oz) | AISC Error (%) |
|------|--------------------------|--------------------|-----------------|--------------------------|--------------------|----------------|
| 2015 | 4.75 | 4.58 | **−3.6%** (MISS) | 990 | 1,025 | **+3.5%** (MISS) |
| 2016 | 4.90 | 4.90 | **0.0%** (HIT) | 910 | 912 | **+0.2%** (HIT) |
| 2017 | 5.20 | 5.27 | **+1.3%** (HIT) | 925 | 924 | **−0.1%** (HIT) |
| 2018 | 5.15 | 5.10 | **−1.0%** (HIT) | 995 | 909 | **−8.6%** (BEAT) |

**Observations:**
- **Production accuracy:** 3 of 4 years within ±1.5% of guidance midpoint. Only 2015 was a clear miss (−3.6%, just outside the guidance range floor of 4.6 Moz)
- **AISC accuracy:** 3 of 4 years were within ±0.5% of guidance midpoint. 2018 was a large beat (−8.6%), reflecting aggressive cost reduction outperformance
- **Bias:** NEM showed a modest tendency to beat on AISC (deliver lower costs than guided) and slight tendency to miss on production in earlier years
- **Pre-2019 company was lean:** standalone NEM was a disciplined, mid-size producer (4.6–5.3 Moz range) with strong operational track record
