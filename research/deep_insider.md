## Section 6: Rule 10b5-1 Plan Verification — How to Confirm

A Rule 10b5-1 plan is confirmed by:
1. **Form 4 checkbox:** Box 3 ("Check this box to indicate that a transaction was made pursuant to a contract, instruction, or written plan for the purchase or sale of equity securities... intended to satisfy the affirmative defense conditions of Rule 10b5-1(c)") must be checked on the actual SEC Form 4.
2. **Footnote disclosure:** The Form 4 must include a footnote such as: *"The sale reported in this Form 4 was effected pursuant to a Rule 10b5-1 trading plan dated [DATE]."*
